A poem makes different moods

"Moments"

Each day brings new moments to see,
some we hold close, some we set free,
and some we don’t grasp till the end of the day,
when time shows their meaning in its own way.

Some moments we quickly forget,
while others, in hearts, are kept set.
Some memories we treasure as gold,
even if their colors grow old.

Each moment’s a gift, small and true,
a piece of time that slips from view.
Maybe that’s why they mean so much –
they come and go, just out of touch.
